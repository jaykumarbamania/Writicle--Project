Ganesh Chaturthi 2021: Here are some iconic temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha that you must visit

Posted : 6 hours ago

Ganesh Chaturthi, one of the most important festivals of the Hindu tradition, will be celebrated on September 10, 2021, across the country.

Ganesh Chaturthi 2021: Here are some iconic temples dedicated to the Lord Ganesha that you must visit

Lord Ganesha, also known as 'Ekadanta', 'Vinayaka', 'Ganpati', is believed to be the one who brings happiness and prosperity for everyone and is the destroyer of obstacles. On Ganesh Chaturthi, devotees perform Ganesh Puja at home and also visit temples to worship Lord Ganesha.

India is home to several ancient temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha. Apart from the famous Siddhivinayak Temple in Maharashtra, these temples, well-known for their architecture and history, find a special mention and must be visited at least once in a lifetime.

Here are some iconic temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha: 1. Siddhivinayak Temple, Mumbai: The Siddhivinayak temple is one of the most famous Ganesh temples in Mumbai and is thronged by a large number of devotees and celebrities every year. The iconic temple was established in 1801 and it is believed that if you truly wish something, then it is granted here. Built by Laxman Vithu Patil, the Siddhivinayak temple is the richest temple in the city and was also visited by Apple CEO Tim Cook in 2016.
